Case study

Meta Omnichannel Ads

Designing a 0-1 online and offline campaign system that turned fragmented offline signals into measurable omnichannel optimization.

Problem
Advertisers needed to optimize campaigns across online and offline outcomes, but offline signals, data integration, and data quality workflows were fragmented across systems.
Role
Led product design strategy and UX architecture for a 0-1 omnichannel campaign product over two years, focusing on offline signals, signal integration, data quality improvement, and campaign decision support.
Design decision
Designed the workflow around signal readiness and advertiser decisions instead of backend setup complexity, helping advertisers understand what data was connected, what quality gaps remained, and how online and offline signals could work together.
Impact
Launched a 0-1 omnichannel campaign product connecting online and offline signals, reaching $1.3B ARR within six months.

Some implementation details and internal workflows are omitted or generalized due to confidentiality. I can share more about my role, process, and responsibilities in interviews.
