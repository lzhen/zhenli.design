Case study

Meta Omnichannel Ads

Designing a 0-1 online and offline campaign system that turned fragmented offline signals into measurable omnichannel optimization.

Problem
Advertisers were investing across online and offline channels, but campaign setup, signal integration, and measurement often lived in disconnected workflows. Offline conversions, store activity, CRM data, and data quality signals were hard to connect back to campaign optimization.
Role
Led product design strategy and UX architecture for a 0-1 omnichannel campaign product, shaping the workflow for offline signal integration, data quality improvement, campaign setup, and cross-channel decision support.
Design decision
Designed the experience around signal readiness and advertiser decisions instead of backend setup complexity, helping advertisers understand what data was connected, what quality gaps remained, and how online and offline signals could work together.
Scope
Offline signals, signal integration, data quality improvement, campaign setup, optimization guidance, and measurement surfaces across online and offline business outcomes.
Impact

Helped launch a 0-1 omnichannel campaign product connecting online and offline signals, reaching $1.3B ARR within six months.

Created a clearer decision framework for advertisers adopting offline-signal workflows and cross-channel optimization.
